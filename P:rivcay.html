Privacy Policy

At Stern Grove Festival ("we," "our," or "us"), your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or participate in our photo contest.

 

When you enter the contest or browse our site, we may collect certain personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and ZIP code. If you submit a photo, we'll also collect the image itself, along with any captions or optional category tags you provide. If you choose to share your social media handles or links—for example, to earn bonus contest entries—we'll store those too. Additionally, we may gather non-personal data like your device type, browser information, and site usage statistics through cookies or similar tracking technologies.

We use this information to run and administer the contest, verify eligibility, notify winners, and display your photos in our public contest gallery. We may also share select submissions on our social media channels or use them in future festival marketing, as outlined in our official contest rules. Beyond the contest, we use this data to maintain and improve our website and overall festival experience.

 

We want to be clear: we do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with trusted service providers who help us operate the website, manage the contest, or communicate with you—for example, our web hosting company, email service, or contest platform. In some cases, we may also share information with marketing partners or sponsors (like Levi's) in direct relation to this contest and festival promotions.

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as the ability to access, update, or delete your information. You can also opt out of marketing emails anytime by clicking the unsubscribe link included at the bottom of our emails. If you'd like to make a data request or have any privacy-related questions, please email us at [info@sterngrove.org].

 

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse, though no online platform can ever be completely secure. Our site may use cookies or similar technologies to improve functionality and analyze traffic. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer. Please note, our website may also contain links to other sites—such as Instagram—and we aren't responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

 

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll post a clear notice on this page. By using our site and participating in our contest, you agree to this policy and our practices.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to us anytime at info@sterngrove.org. Thank you for being a part of the Stern Grove Festival community!
